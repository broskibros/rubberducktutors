Ready to get started?

Here's how it works!

It's quick, simple and easy to start learning with us.

Introduce yourself

Reach out through the contact form, and you'll be able to book a free introductory session. This will allow us to get to know each other by discussing your previous experience, your goals, and what you are looking for in a tutor. Together, we’ll decide on a starting point and will recommend which of our tutors is the best fit for your unique needs.

Book a session

Following the introductory session, you can book virtual tutoring sessions with any of our tutors. Tutor availability is updated in real-time, and virtual meeting rooms are automatically assigned.

Access your classroom

Between lessons with us, you'll able to access your own private Google classroom. This will be the home of all tutor lesson notes, homework assignments and direct communications with your tutors. This classroom can be kept private, or shared with your whānau - the choice is yours!
